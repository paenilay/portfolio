HUMAN RESOURCE DEPARTMENT DASHBOARD

The dashboard provides management with real-time insights into workforce utilization, attendance patterns, and staffing trends, supporting efficient HR planning and operational decision-making.

Key Demonstration Insights

01 / Workforce Utilization

Enables accurate capacity monitoring by mapping real-time operational seat allocations against unique daily capacity limits. This prevents bottlenecking and ensures optimal team coverage across shifting project baselines.

02 / Attendance Patterns

Tracks historical availability trends, absenteeism baselines, and shift compliance data to pinpoint cyclical operational risks. This insight allows management to proactively address scheduling friction before it affects delivery timelines.

03 / Staffing Trends & Planning

Surfaces structural resource gaps and attrition signals across departments, giving leadership clear visibility into future staffing demands. These metrics turn reactive hiring into a planned, business-aligned strategy.
